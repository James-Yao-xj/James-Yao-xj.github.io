This is an essay done by my classmate Ziheng He and I. We spent 4 days together to estimate the resource consumption of moving 100,000 people from the Earth to the Moon. The figures in this essay come from official websites, including NASA and so on, thus, we wish that this essay can really make a sense and help people understand the difficulty of colonizing our neighbours. We should also realise how precious our mother earth is, and always try our best to protect our planet.
