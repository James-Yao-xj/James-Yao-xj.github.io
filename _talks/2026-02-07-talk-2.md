This was a presentation I gave to 12th graders who were preparing for the College Entrance Exam. I shared some of my personal stories with the students. The presentation was also recorded and shown in many other schools, especially in remote areas. I also had the chance to answer questions from the audience. It was a truly wonderful morning spent at my alma mater, and it was a great honor to be able to give this presentation!
