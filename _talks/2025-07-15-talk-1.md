As I was admitted to Peking University, my high school teacher invited me to deliver a talk at Chengdu East New District No.7 Middle School. During the talk, I discussed the key differences between high school and junior high school, and offered suggestions on developing effective habits for high school life. I also shared personal stories from my own high school experience, using interesting examples to illustrate my points. The session ended with a Q&A, where I answered students' questions. The atmosphere was fantastic, and I truly enjoyed the afternoon.
