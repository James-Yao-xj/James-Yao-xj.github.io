As I was accepted to PKU, my high school teacher invited me to give a talk in Chengdu East New District No.7 Middle School. I talked about the differences between high school and junior high school, then I gave suggestions on some habbits in high school. I also shared my personal stories in high school, giving them some interesting cases to demonstrate my points. I also answered the questions. The atmosphere was very great and I enjoyed a great afternoon.
